Frequently Asked Questions

What is the men's dress code?

Black or midnight blue jacket and trousers, white dress shirt, black bow tie, and black leather shoes. Optional: cummerbund or waistcoat, cufflinks, and pocket square.

What time should I arrive?

Check-in opens from 2:00 PM. Once you're settled, please join us downstairs for a welcome toast — it's our way to kick off the celebrations together!

Do we need to get ready before arriving?

Yes please - we'd love everyone to arrive dressed up and ready for the festivities. That way, we can go straight into enjoying ourselves without too much rushing around.

Is there parking available?

Yes, complimentary parking is available on the grounds of Hedsor House. Please follow the signs upon arrival.

How to get to Hedsor House?

By Car:
From London Centre: ~45 minutes
From Heathrow: ~25 minutes

Public Transport:
Elizabeth Line (from Heathrow or London Centre) → Taplow station, then a short cab ride

Most recommended way: cab or by car, but if you're arriving via public transport, please get in touch and we will arrange pick up from Taplow.

Do I need a visa to visit the UK?

For Malaysian and Singaporean citizens:
Apply for UK ETA online before your trip

For other nationalities:
You may need to apply for a UK Standard Visitor Visa in advance. Check your requirements on the UK Government website.

We recommend applying early to avoid any last-minute stress!
