About Hedsor House

Nestled in the heart of Buckinghamshire, Hedsor House is a magnificent Georgian country estate surrounded by 100 acres of stunning parkland and gardens. This historic venue provides the perfect backdrop for our special day, combining timeless elegance with natural beauty.
