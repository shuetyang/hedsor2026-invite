Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

Formal attire is requested. Gentlemen are asked to wear suits or morning dress, and ladies are invited to wear cocktail dresses or formal day wear.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at Hedsor House by 1:30 PM. The ceremony will begin promptly at 2:00 PM.

Is there parking available?

Yes, complimentary parking is available on the grounds of Hedsor House. Please follow the signs upon arrival.

Can I bring children?

While we love your little ones, we have chosen to make our wedding an adults-only celebration to allow all guests to relax and enjoy the evening.

What about accommodation?

We have arranged special rates at several local hotels. Please contact us for recommendations and booking information.

Is there a gift registry?

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift. However, if you wish to give something, we would be grateful for a contribution towards our honeymoon fund.
