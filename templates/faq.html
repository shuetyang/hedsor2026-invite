Wedding Details & Information

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at Hedsor House by 3:30 PM to allow time for seating and getting settled. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00 PM in the grand drawing room. Late arrivals may not be admitted during the ceremony.

How do I get to Hedsor House?

Hedsor House is located in Buckinghamshire, approximately 30 minutes from London. The nearest train station is Taplow (Great Western Railway), and we recommend arranging transportation from there. There is ample free parking available on-site for those driving. Detailed directions will be provided with your invitation.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is formal attire. For gentlemen: morning suit, lounge suit, or formal dark suit with tie. For ladies: cocktail dress, formal day dress, or smart separates. Please avoid wearing white or ivory to avoid confusion with the bride. Hats are welcome but not required.

Are children welcome?

While we adore your little ones, we have chosen to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. This allows all guests to relax and enjoy the evening without worrying about childcare. We appreciate your understanding and look forward to celebrating with you!

There will be an open bar during the reception serving champagne, wine, beer, and spirits. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know in your RSVP.

Can I take photos?

We kindly ask that you refrain from taking photos during the ceremony to maintain the solemn atmosphere. During the reception, feel free to take photos and share them! We'll also have a professional photographer capturing all the special moments throughout the day.

Where are you registered?

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift! However, if you'd like to give something, we have registries at John Lewis and Liberty London. You can also contribute to our honeymoon fund. We're planning to honeymoon in the Scottish Highlands.

Is there parking available?

Yes! There is plenty of free parking available at Hedsor House. We also recommend carpooling or using ride-sharing services if you plan to enjoy the open bar. The venue is also accessible by taxi from Taplow station.

What if it rains?

Hedsor House has beautiful indoor spaces that will accommodate all our guests regardless of the weather. The ceremony will take place in the grand drawing room, and the reception will be held in the elegant reception rooms. The gardens are available for photos if the weather permits.

When does the reception end?

The reception will end at 11:00 PM. We'll have a grand exit celebration in the gardens (weather permitting), and then we'll all head home with wonderful memories of our special day at Hedsor House!
